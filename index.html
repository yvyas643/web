ABOUT Nisyog

A blood bank service which was Inaugurated on 19/05/2002 by Deendayal upadhyay
NisYog enforces Drug & Cosmetic Act, National blood policy standards and guidelines ensuring proper collection & donation of blood under an effective management and monitoring the quality and quantity of the donated blood. Considering the necessity by hospitals and private outlets in need, NiYog has been developed to easily incorporate specific requirements from nationwide
